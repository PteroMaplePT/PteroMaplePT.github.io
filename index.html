About Me

Research Interests

I am particularly interested in automated prompting methods that induce effective prompting strategies from a small number of examples, reducing the high cost of manual prompt engineering.

Research Highlights

My research extends automated prompting methods to both text and multimodal tasks, achieving significant improvements in few-shot learning scenarios.

Instruction Induction

Developing methods to automatically generate effective prompts from minimal examples, reducing manual prompt engineering costs.

Multimodal Learning

Extending instruction induction to visual and audio question answering tasks, bridging text and multimodal domains.

Zero-Shot Reasoning

Investigating multi-rationale approaches to improve zero-shot reasoning capabilities in large language models.
